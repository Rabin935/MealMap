    • Healthy Gundruk Soup

    Gundruk soup is a traditional and healthy Nepali dish made from fermented leafy greens, typically mustard, radish, or cauliflower leaves. Rich in probiotics and nutrients, it is a treasured comfort food, especially in the colder months. The fermentation process enhances its tangy flavor, while spices like turmeric, garlic, and chili add warmth and depth. Gundruk soup is low in calories and packed with fiber, vitamins, and minerals, making it excellent for digestion and boosting immunity.
