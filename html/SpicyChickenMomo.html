    • Spicy Chicken Momo

    Spicy chicken momo is a popular Nepali dumpling dish, cherished for its rich flavors and fiery kick. Made with minced chicken blended with aromatic spices, garlic, ginger, onions, and fresh coriander, the filling is wrapped in soft dough and steamed or fried to perfection. The highlight of this dish is its spicy dipping sauce, a tangy and zesty tomato-based chutney infused with chili and sesame flavors. Whether served as a snack or a main course, spicy chicken momo offers a perfect balance of heat, tenderness, and savory delight, making it a favorite among food lovers seeking bold and satisfying tastes.

    Preperation Time:

    30 mins

    Cooking Time:

    10 mins

    Total Time:

    40 mins

    Servings:

    2
